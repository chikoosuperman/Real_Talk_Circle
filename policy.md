# Privacy & Usage Policy: The Insight Circle ⭕

## Privacy Commitment
We believe in absolute data sovereignty. Our applications are built with a **"Zero-Data" Architecture**, applicable across all sectors—Health, Wealth, and Beyond.

### 1. Stateless Operations
- None of your input data (biometrics, financial figures, personal planning) is transmitted to any server.
- Calculations are performed entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript.
- No database records, logs, or analytics persist your sensitive information.

### 2. No Tracking
- We do not use third-party tracking cookies, behavioral analysis, or ad-targeting scripts.
- Your session is private, ephemeral, and self-contained.

## Sector-Specific Disclaimers

### 1. Health & Wellness
- **Tool Intent**: General population screening (e.g., BMI, BMR, Macro ratios).
- **Not Medical Advice**: Results are for informational purposes only. Consult highly qualified healthcare professionals for clinical diagnosis or medical treatment.
- **Reference**: Calculations are based on established standards (e.g., WHO, NIH).

### 2. Wealth & Financial Planning
- **Tool Intent**: Mathematical projections and educational simulations (e.g., CAGR, ROI, Loan Amortization).
- **Not Financial Advice**: Our tools do not constitute professional investment, tax, or legal advice.
- **Risk**: Financial markets are volatile. Projections are not guarantees of future performance. Consult a certified financial planner.

### 3. General Strategic Planning
- **Tool Intent**: Logic-based decision support and organizational calculations.
- **Not Professional Strategy**: Tools are meant for brainstorming and high-level modeling. Results should be verified with specific domain experts.
