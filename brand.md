# Brand Guidelines: The Insight Circle ⭕

## Vision & Philosophy
**The Insight Circle ⭕** is a multi-disciplinary brand dedicated to providing professional, high-precision analytics and actionable insights across all facets of life—from **Health** and **Wellness** to **Wealth** and **Financial Planning**. Our philosophy centers on **Minimalist Excellence**, **Absolute Privacy**, and **Scientific or Mathematical Accuracy**.

## Core Values
1.  **Precision**: Every calculation adheres to established global standards (e.g., WHO for health, GAAP or industry-leading financial formulas for wealth).
2.  **Privacy**: A "Zero-Data" approach—no storage, no tracking, just utility.
3.  **Clarity**: Complex data (biological or financial) presented with elegant, intuitive visuals.
4.  **Professionalism**: Tools designed for serious analysis, delivered with premium aesthetics.

## Visual Identity

### Design System: Glassmorphism
The signature aesthetic for all "Insight Circle" products is **Glassmorphism**—feeling like light interacting with glass: layered, blurred, and sophisticated.
- **Backdrop Blur**: `12px` to `16px`.
- **Borders**: `1px` solid, low opacity (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`).
- **Shadows**: Soft, deep shadows for depth (`0 20px 50px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3)`).
- **Theme**: Dark Mode Primary (`#0d1117`).

### Sector-Specific Themes
While the design system remains consistent, sector-specific accents help users identify the context:

| Sector | Primary Accent | Visual Motif |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health & Wellness** | Teal (`#2fb9ad`) | Pulse/Heartbeat line SVG |
| **Wealth & Finance** | Gold/Yellow (`#d29922`) | Currency symbols / Growth charts |
| **Strategic Planning** | Blue (`#388bfd`) | Pathway/Nodes / Connection lines |
| **Alerts/Critical** | Red (`#f85149`) | Sharp edges / High contrast pulses |

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
- **Hierarchy**: Bold headers for conclusions, medium weight for labels, and light body text for context.

## Logo & Iconography
- **Visual Motif**: The "Insight Circle" is represented by the ⭕ emoji or a perfect, thin-stroke circle.
- **Logo Usage**: Centered or top-aligned, often paired with a sector-specific icon (e.g., a pulse for health, a coin/chart for wealth).
